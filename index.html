Conjectory

I made an automated conjecture finder conjectory.org . This essentially detects whenever a sequence on OEIS is strictly contained in another sequence and then renders a graph where the arrow in the graph represents a conjecture. I would like to eventually scale this out as part of something I call the "moonshine project" which would consist of a collection of automated systems for detecting new conjectures that are meaningful to study, the name "moonshine" originating from this .
